About Us

Parking Spot Finder is a web application that leverages the power of AI and OpenCV to help users find available parking spots in real-time. Our intelligent system analyzes camera footage and provides accurate information about parking availability, making it easier for drivers to find parking spaces quickly and conveniently.

For more information or any inquiries, please contact us using the information provided on the Contact Us page.
